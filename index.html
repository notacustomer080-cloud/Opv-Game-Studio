About Us

Opv Game Studios is an independent game studio made by one single person who creates horror games and tries to make entertainment good and accessible again.

Here at Opv Game Studios, we make projects and keep them alive with constant updates all the time. Our games are mostly distributed on itch.io and Steam.

We focus on dark atmospheres, retro PSX-style visuals, and experiences that feel mysterious, scary, and memorable.
